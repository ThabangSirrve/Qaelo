Question: What is Qaelo?
Answer: Qaelo pronounced “Khayelo” is a freelancing website for students, graduates and young professionals, this platform allows freelancers to choose up to 3 out of more than 60 freelance options in order make money, build their CV’s and create a business from. With more than 2000 universities, Qaelo also allows the free listing of rooms, shops, events and groups per university in order to help students, graduates and young professionals find the best deals in town.
What is a freelancer?
Answer: A freelancer is a self-employed person that offers services to organisations, companies and the general community at a price set by the self-employed person.

Examples…
– Can you make people laugh? Through Qaelo you can freelance (offer a service as a self-employed person) as a comedian and charge organisations, companies and the general community for your services when they hire you.
– Can you look after children? Through Qaelo you can freelance (offer a service as a self-employed person) as a babysitter and charge parents for looking after their kids as they go out to dinner or late events.
– Are you good in accounting? Through Qaelo you can freelance (offer a service as a self-employed person) as a bookkeeper and help start-ups and small businesses with their tax returns etc.

Question: Who is Qaelo meant for?
Answer:
1. Qaelo is meant for students, graduates and young professionals as a channel for them to earn extra income from freelance activities.
2. This service is also meant for the community, both the business and household communities to find affordable freelance service providers such as babysitters and more.
3. Thirdly, Qaelo is meant for property landlords and agents, shop owners and event managers to display, inform and advertise specials to students, graduates and young professionals about their products and services.
4. Lastly, Qaelo is meant for students, graduates and young professionals who’d like to create and run groups also known as Fraternities or student organisations.

Question: How do I advertise myself on landlord’s profiles?
Answer: After you have signed in to your freelance profile, you will notice a button titled ADVERTISE TO LANDLORDS, click that button and complete the process.

Question: How much is it to advertise on Landlord’s profile and how does it work?
Answer:
• It is completely free to advertise yourself to landlords.
• This functionality is a way to help you find a room to rent easier because your free ad will mention how much rent you are willing to pay, your contact details and other useful information that the property manager will need to see if you qualify to stay in their property.

Question: What is a Shop profile and what are the benefits associated with creating one?
Answer: A shop profile is an online profile of a shop that is at most 10 kilometres (6 miles) away from a higher institution of learning. The profile should have:
• A picture of the shop
• Closing and opening times
• A short description
• A menu or product list items for sale
• Location of the shop and
• Contact information

Benefits of creating a shop profile

• It is completely free!
• You can list as many shops as you own near a university under 1 profile
• You can create a special and send it to all the freelance profiles in the university you serve
• You can edit and delete a shop profile you have created

Question: What is an Event profile and what are the benefits associated with creating one?
Answer: An event profile is an online profile of an event that is at most 10 kilometres (6 miles) away from a higher institution of learning. The profile should have:
• A Picture of the proposed event
• Detailed description of the event
• Location of the event
• Contact information

Benefits of creating an event profile

• It is completely free!
• You can list different events near a university or different universities under 1 profile!
• A feature for setting up a ticket gateway where you will sell your event tickets through us is coming in the near future!
• You can edit or delete your event profile anytime

Question: What is a Group profile and what are the benefits associated with creating one?
Answer: A group profile is an online profile of a group AKA fraternity or student organisation (registered or not) in a higher institution of learning. The profile should have:
• A picture of the group team or logo of the group
• Detailed description about the group
• Office location of the group
• Contact information

Benefits of creating a group profile

• It is completely free!
• You can build an online list for your group by encouraging them to click the blue join button on your group profile
• You can list events under your group profile
• You can edit or delete your group profile anytime
