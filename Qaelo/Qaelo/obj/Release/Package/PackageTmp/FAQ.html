FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions


GENERAL

Question: What is Qaelo?
Answer: Answer: Qaelo, pronounced “Khayelo” is a student website that allows the listing of student accommodation rooms, events, shops, societies and more free!

Question: How can I contact Qaelo for any enquiries?
Answer: Please scroll down to the end of our home page or email us at info@qaelo.com

Question: How do I go about advertising on Qaelo?
Answer: Please send a request to advertise to advertise@qaelo.com and one of our representatives will be in contact with you!

CREATING A PROFILE


Question: I want to create a Qaelo profile, what is each category meant for so that I may post on the relevant category and how much will it cost me?
Answer: The following are the categories we have, what they are meant for. Please note that it is completely free to create a profile with us!

Student accommodation (These are private or off-campus houses meant for students)
Events (This platform allows you to post an event and also sell tickets if you so wish)
Student jobs job profile (Students who want to work for the institution part time or full time and whose information will only be seen by the University)
Shops (Any business within, close to or next to a Higher Institution, it can be a retail store like a Clothing store or it can be a service shop like a Hair Salon, Gym and etc.)
Student Societies (Any registered student organisation).
HR department (Companies posting jobs for students to apply)

Question: Can I change the information that I put on my Qaelo Profile?
Answer: Yes, all you have to do is to login to your profile and change what you want to change.
